— Questions & Answers —

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay monthly after the one-time fee?

No. Our packages are a one-time payment. The only ongoing cost is domain renewal after the first free year (typically ₹800–1,200/year), which you pay directly to the domain registrar.

How does the process work after I pay?

After payment, we send you a simple questionnaire about your business, brand colours, and content. Once we have your details, we start building immediately. You'll see a preview before we go live.

Can I upgrade from Starter to Business later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade at any time by paying the difference. We'll build on top of your existing site so nothing is wasted.

What if I don't have content or images ready?

We help you structure your content and can source relevant stock images at no extra charge. Many clients give us a rough brief and we write the copy for them.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

Yes — every single site we build is mobile-first. We test on real Android and iOS devices before handover. Over 70% of Indian web traffic is mobile, so this is non-negotiable for us.

How do I make changes to the website after it's live?

Simply WhatsApp us. During the free support period, changes are free. After that, we offer a simple pay-per-change model or a monthly retainer for regular updates.
